How to Play ZType Typing Game

Playing our ZType game is simple but challenging. The ZType mechanics are easy to learn:

1. Enemy wordships will descend from the top of the screen in ZType carrying words

2. Type the word shown on the ZType ship and press spacebar to fire

3. Destroy the ZType ship before it reaches the bottom of the screen

4. The ZType game gets progressively faster and more difficult

5. Track your Words Per Minute (WPM), accuracy, and ZType score
