About Us

The story behind our typing tool

Typer.app began with a simple idea: to make learning and practicing typing a fun and effective experience. As a student myself, I realized how crucial it is to improve typing speed and accuracy, but traditional methods can often be boring. That's why I decided to create a platform where the learning process could be exciting and engaging.

This website is my effort to help fellow students and anyone looking to improve their typing skills. I built it on my own, but its purpose is to empower a larger community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make typing easier, faster, and more enjoyable for everyone. We aim to provide a tool that not only boosts your WPM (Words Per Minute) but also turns the habit of typing into a positive experience.

Our Future Plans

This is just the beginning. We are continuously improving Typer.app and plan to add more features, including:

  • New and exciting typing games.
  • More advanced practice materials and tutorials.
  • A community forum where users can connect and share their progress.

Thank you for joining us on this journey. With every keystroke, you're getting closer to mastery!
